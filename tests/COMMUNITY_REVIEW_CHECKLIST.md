# Community Review Checklist

After trying any skill in this library, use this checklist to share your honest feedback. No technical knowledge needed — just your professional judgement.

Fill this in and paste it into a [Quality Report](https://github.com/ur-grue/autopunk-media-skills/issues/new?template=quality-report.md) issue on GitHub.

---

## Five Questions

**1. Could you use this output in your work without major editing?**

- [ ] Yes — I could use it as-is or with only light touch-ups
- [ ] Partially — I could use parts of it, but significant sections needed rewriting
- [ ] No — I had to start over from scratch

---

**2. Did the output stay on topic and actually address what you asked for?**

- [ ] Yes — it focused on my input and didn't drift
- [ ] Mostly — there were some irrelevant sections, but the core was on target
- [ ] No — it went off in a different direction or ignored key parts of my input

---

**3. Did the output invent or misrepresent any facts, names, or details you provided?**

- [ ] No — everything in the output can be traced back to what I gave it
- [ ] Minor issues — small inaccuracies that were easy to spot and fix
- [ ] Yes — it made things up or got important details wrong

---

**4. Did the output match the format described in the skill (length, structure, sections)?**

- [ ] Yes — the format was exactly as described
- [ ] Mostly — minor formatting differences, nothing that affected usability
- [ ] No — the format was significantly different from what the skill promised

---

**5. Would you recommend this skill to a colleague in your field?**

- [ ] Yes, without hesitation
- [ ] Yes, with a caveat (explain below)
- [ ] No
