*Ideal Publication:* Economic policy feature, labour desk

*Ideal Publication:* Management/leadership feature, business magazine
*Pitch Line:* "Everyone talks about what the four-day week means for workers and CEOs. Nobody has asked the middle managers who have to make it actually function."
*Timeframe:* Evergreen
