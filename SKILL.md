# Autopunk Media Skills

The most comprehensive Claude skill library for media professionals. 394 skills across 15 categories, each tested against a 7-dimension G-Eval quality framework with a 4.0/5 minimum bar.

## What This Skill Collection Does

Provides ready-to-use Claude skills for every stage of media production: from story ideation and research through scripting, post-production, distribution, and business operations.

## Categories

- **TV & Documentary** — treatments, scripts, shoot schedules, press kits, co-production briefs
- **Magazine Journalism** — story angles, headlines, fact-checking, FOIA requests, copy editing
- **YouTube** — SEO titles, hooks, scripts, thumbnails, channel strategy, sponsorship pitches
- **Podcast** — episode concepts, guest research, show notes, ad reads, media kits
- **Screenwriting** — loglines, treatments, beat sheets, scene writing, coverage reports
- **Image Prompting** — Midjourney, Flux, art direction, visual identity, cover pipelines
- **Social Media** — captions, threads, carousels, short-form video, hashtag strategy
- **Newsletter** — edition writing, subject lines, content arcs, referral copy, paid tiers
- **PR & Communications** — press releases, crisis statements, spokesperson briefs, op-eds
- **Radio & Audio** — bulletin scripts, presenter links, documentary narration, running orders
- **Data Journalism** — data story finding, chart descriptions, methodology explainers, FOI data
- **Research** — biography briefs, legislative history, scientific paper summaries, travel logistics
- **Translation** — article translation, press release translation, glossary building, quality review
- **Production Support** — CMS metadata, manuscript formatting, fact sheets, author bios
- **Media Business** — investor decks, grant applications, festival submissions, contract summaries

## Installation

```bash
npx autopunk-media-skills install --all
```

Or browse and install individual skills from the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/ur-grue/autopunk-media-skills).

## Quality Standard

Every stable skill passes a 7-dimension G-Eval with a 4.0/5 minimum threshold, including an Editorial Naturalness hard floor that catches AI-sounding output.
